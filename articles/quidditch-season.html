Quidditch Season Kicks Off with a Thrilling Match

The Quidditch season officially began today with an electrifying match between the Holyhead Harpies and the Puddlemere United. The game, held at the newly renovated Quidditch stadium in Yorkshire, drew a massive crowd of cheering fans. The Harpies emerged victorious with a final score of 250-180 after a nail-biting chase for the Golden Snitch.

Star Seeker Ginny Weasley of the Holyhead Harpies caught the Snitch in a spectacular dive, securing the win for her team. "It was a tough game, but we played our hearts out," Weasley commented after the match. The season promises to be one of the most competitive yet, with several teams strengthening their rosters over the summer.

By Ludo Bagman
